üé® Icon Design Tips:

  • Use simple, recognizable shapes
  • Keep a consistent stroke width (1.5-2px works well)
  • Design on a 16x16 or 24x24 grid
  • Use theme colors: #8564d8 (purple), #00d4ff (cyan), etc.
  • Ensure good contrast against dark backgrounds
